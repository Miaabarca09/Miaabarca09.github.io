Directions for the chile

  1. toast chiles on a pan
  2. startplacing chile in a siplck bag one by one after toasted
  3. after the chiles skin is removed cut it open and remove the seeds
  4. Get your meat and season it ith the seasonings you perfer
  5. The add the meat once its ready inside the chile and add cheese
  6. To hold the chile from spillng the filling place a tooth pick through the chile
  7. Beat the eggs in a bowl
  8. Dip each chile into the egg wash to coat the chile
  9. Fry each chile in the oil to get it nice and toasted

Directions for salsa

  1. Add tomatoes andchile to boil in a pot
  2. in a blender add the chiles,garlic,onions,sat,and peper into a blender and blend
  3. Add a little bit of oil to a pot then add the salsa in so it can warm up

How to combine

  1. After the salsa comes to a boil add the chiles to the salsa
  2. If wanted you can add sour cream as a topping while plating
